About me

I am a web developer student who is passionate about creating beautiful and functional websites. I am currently Information Science at University of Maryland and have gained experience in various programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Pyhton and R.

I am also proficient in using Adobe Creative Suite, including Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign Premiere, Dreamweaver and Figma. I have a deep understanding of how to use these tools to design and create eye-catching graphics and layouts for websites. I also understand how to use them to manipulate and optimize images, which is essential in creating a user-friendly and visually appealing website.

Moreover, I have experience working with content management systems like WordPress and Wix, which have allowed me to create and manage websites with ease. I have a strong understanding of how to install and customize themes, as well as how to use plugins to enhance the functionality of a website.

I am always eager to learn new skills and techniques and believe in the importance of keeping up with the latest trends in web development. I am confident that my skills and experience, combined with my passion for web development, make me a valuable asset to any team.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about me. I am excited to share my portfolio with you and look forward to the opportunity to show you my skills in action.
